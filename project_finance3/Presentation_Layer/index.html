Automated Cash Flow Forecasting

One client needed better visibility into liquidity without spending hours pulling data manually. We built an AI-driven solution that:

Pulls accounts receivable and payable directly from their ERP
Starts with current bank balances
Incorporates average client payment history and vendor terms
Factors in recurring inflows/outflows and one-time items
Generates accurate short-term (13-week) and long-term (12-month) rolling forecasts

The result: leadership now has a reliable cash position view updated daily — enabling confident decisions on hiring, capital expenditures, and credit lines.
