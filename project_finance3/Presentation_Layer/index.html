Case Study 1

Automated Monthly Bank Reconciliation for a Mid-Sized Retailer (NetSuite)

Problem Definition

A growing retailer with multiple locations faced delays in month-end close due to manual bank reconciliations in NetSuite. Teams downloaded statements and exports, then matched transactions in spreadsheets—typically 10–15 hours per account monthly, leading to errors and late variance discovery.

Solution & Implementation

We built a custom Node.js (Express) backend using NetSuite SuiteTalk REST API for secure pulls, integrated bank feeds, and an AI matching engine (TensorFlow.js) that auto-matched 95%+ transactions and flagged exceptions with audit trails.

Rollout included OAuth, mapping, testing, a secure review dashboard, then multi-account scaling with configurable tolerances. Post-launch tweaks shipped quickly as new edge cases emerged.

Case Study 3: Advanced Project Forecasting and EAC Management (Integrated with Dynamics 365)
Problem Definition

Project managers in a mid-sized engineering and construction firm relied on manual spreadsheets to compile actual costs, forecast remaining expenses, and calculate Estimate at Completion (EAC). This process consumed 8–12 hours per month per project, leading to outdated insights, delayed variance detection, and inaccurate revenue recognition under percentage-of-completion methods. Without real-time integration to Dynamics 365, forecasts were prone to mismatched data and errors, hindering proactive decisions and risking overruns.

Solution & Implementation

We developed a custom EAC and project planning system integrated with Dynamics 365 to pull real-time actual costs (labor, materials, equipment, subcontractors, commitments). PMs entered forward-looking forecasts for planned hours and direct costs, while the system loaded indirects, applied configurable revenue recognition formulas (e.g., cost-to-cost POC), and calculated projected revenue, margin, and EAC.

Key Capabilities
  • Interactive charts and graphs (burn rates, variance trends).
  • Side-by-side comparisons: forecasts vs. Dynamics 365 actuals.
  • Exception reporting and variance alerts for early overrun detection.
  • Anomaly detection for unusual spending patterns.
  • Commentary sections for narrative explanations on variances.
  • Mobile-accessible dashboards for on-the-go updates.
Built with a Node.js backend for ERP integration/data processing and a responsive React frontend for intuitive PM workflows.
Tech Details
  • Weeks 1–2: Secure Dynamics 365 integration, mapping, and forecasting UI prototype.
  • Weeks 3–4: EAC calculations, revenue engine, graphs, usability iterations.
  • Weeks 5–6: Alerts, anomaly rules, commentary tools, mobile optimizations, notifications.
  • Weeks 7–8: Training, tuning, go-live with parallel testing vs. manual processes.
Impact
Reduced monthly forecasting time from 8–12 hours to under 2 hours per project.
Annual time savings: 110+ hours per project.
Overrun reduction: estimated 15–20% from earlier intervention on variances.
Estimated savings: $11,000+ per project annually.
Improved revenue recognition compliance and overall profitability via real-time, data-driven insights.

Case Study 6: Intelligent Accounts Receivable Monitoring and Collections Acceleration (Integrated with Acumatica)
Problem Definition

A growing service-based company struggled with elevated DSO due to manual weekly exports and aging reports. Tracking overdue invoices consumed 6–10 hours per week, causing delayed follow-ups, inconsistent escalation, and prolonged payment cycles. Without real-time visibility or predictive insights, collections stayed reactive—creating cash flow constraints and higher bad debt risk.

Solution & Implementation

We implemented a real-time AR monitoring system with continuous synchronization from Acumatica, providing instant access to aging, invoices, and customer payment histories. The system added predictive baselines, automated escalation workflows, AI-driven prioritization, and multi-channel collaboration (Slack/email, dispute tracking, commentary logs).

Key Enhancements
  • Historical analysis & predictive insights: customer-specific payment baselines and delay prediction.
  • Automated escalation workflows: proactive outreach and progressive follow-ups (reminders → PM/collections involvement).
  • AI-driven prioritization: risk scoring by history, age, amount, and behavior; prioritized queues.
  • Multi-channel communication: Slack/email notifications, dispute tracking, shared notes.
  • Best-practice features: dashboards, aging visualizations, exception alerts, incentive tracking, fast dispute tools.
Tech Details
  • Weeks 1–3: Acumatica API sync, historical import, baseline dashboards, real-time monitoring foundation.
  • Weeks 4–5: Risk scoring model, predictive delay detection, queues, escalation rules with feedback.
  • Week 6: Slack/email integrations, dispute tracking, outreach templates, analytics rollout.
  • Weeks 7–8: Training, workflow testing, go-live with parallel monitoring.
Impact
Reduced manual tracking time by 90%+ (320+ hours annually).
Improved DSO by 20–30% via earlier identification and resolution of delays.
Estimated annual savings: $32,000+ (labor, reduced financing needs, minimized bad debt risk).
Improved cash predictability and stronger client relationships through proactive communication.
Case Study 7: Driver-Based Budgeting and Advanced Scenario Forecasting (Integrated with Infor)
Problem Definition

A mid-sized manufacturing and distribution company used complex quarterly spreadsheets for budgeting/forecasting. Each cycle required 20–30 hours of manual entry and consolidation, leaving little time for scenario planning. Volatility in materials, labor, and demand made the lack of flexible what-if capabilities a major constraint.

Solution & Implementation

We built a custom FP&A platform integrated with Infor to pull real-time actuals, commitments, and operational drivers. Users input key drivers and the system generated projections across P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow, enabling unlimited scenarios, collaboration, and AI-assisted baselines with anomaly highlighting.

Key Features
  • Unlimited scenarios with side-by-side comparisons.
  • Real-time collaboration with version tracking, commentary, and audit trails.
  • AI-assisted forecasting from historical trends and driver correlations.
  • Dashboards with variance analysis, sensitivity analysis, and what-if simulations.
  • Rolling forecasts and variance alerts.
  • Validation controls to prevent formula errors and ensure data integrity.
Tech Details
  • Weeks 1–3: Infor integration, historical import, driver engine build, validation controls, prototype.
  • Weeks 4–5: Scenario management + collaboration features; usability iterations.
  • Week 6: Reporting suite, advanced what-if simulations, AI module, dashboards rollout.
  • Weeks 7–8: Training, performance optimization, parallel testing vs. spreadsheets, deployment.
Impact
Reduced quarterly budgeting time from 20–30 hours to under 8 hours per cycle.
Annual time savings: 115+ hours.
Better scenario planning and faster, data-driven decisions in volatile conditions.
Estimated annual savings: $11,500+.
Reduced errors and improved cross-department alignment, positioning finance as a proactive strategic partner.
