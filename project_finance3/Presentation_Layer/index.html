Automated Cash Flow Forecasting

One client needed better visibility into liquidity without spending hours pulling data manually. We built an AI-driven solution that:

Starts with current bank balances. Pulls accounts receivable and payable directly from their ERP. Incorporates average client payment history and vendor terms. Factors in recurring inflows/outflows and one-time items. Generates accurate short-term (13-week) and long-term (12-month) rolling forecasts.

The result: a reliable cash position view updated daily.
